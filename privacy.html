Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 7, 2026

1. Introduction & Scope

This Privacy Policy describes how The Play Kit ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, protects, and discloses your personal information when you visit our website or purchase our digital product bundles. As the data controller of your personal data, we are committed to upholding the highest standards of data privacy. This policy applies strictly to all visitors, users, and buyers of The Play Kit.

2. Children’s Privacy (COPPA & GDPR-K Compliance)

CRITICAL NOTICE: While The Play Kit provides digital storybooks and coloring books designed for the educational benefit and enjoyment of children aged 3 to 7, our website, marketing, and commercial services are strictly intended for adults (parents, legal guardians, and educators).

In strict compliance with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) in the United States, and the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) in the European Union, we unequivocally state that we do not knowingly solicit, collect, or process personal data from anyone under the age of 13 (or under 16 in certain EU jurisdictions). If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your minor child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately. Upon verification, we will take immediate, irreversible steps to securely delete such information from our systems and our processors' systems.

3. Data Collection & Third-Party Processors

To provide our services, we adhere to the principle of data minimization, collecting only the exact data required to fulfill our contractual obligations to you.

  • Information We Collect: When you make a purchase or contact support, we collect your name and email address. This is utilized exclusively to deliver your digital files, send receipt confirmations, and provide customer support.
  • Financial Information & Merchant of Record: We do not collect, process, or store your credit card numbers, bank details, or complex billing information. All financial transactions, billing details, and tax compliance (including VAT/Sales tax collection) are entirely managed by our authorized Merchant of Record, Lemon Squeezy. Lemon Squeezy processes your payment utilizing secure, 256-bit encrypted gateways. We strongly advise reviewing Lemon Squeezy’s own Privacy Policy regarding how they manage your sensitive financial data.

4. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Our website utilizes cookies and similar tracking technologies to ensure site functionality and measure performance.

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the website to function properly, including routing you to our secure checkout. These cannot be disabled in our systems.
  • Analytics & Performance: We may use aggregated, anonymized data to understand how visitors interact with our site (e.g., measuring page views). This data cannot be used to identify you personally.
  • Advertising & Retargeting: We partner with third-party advertising networks (including Meta/Facebook) to display relevant advertisements on other platforms. These platforms utilize tracking pixels and cookies to measure ad performance and deliver targeted marketing based on your interaction with our site. You can adjust your ad preferences directly within your social media account settings.

You have the right to accept or decline non-essential cookies. Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can universally modify your browser settings to decline cookies if you prefer.

5. Global Privacy Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

Depending on your geographic location, including residents of the European Economic Area (EEA), the United Kingdom, and California (under the CCPA), you are guaranteed specific, enforceable rights regarding your personal data:

  • The Right to Access: You may request copies of your personal data held by us.
  • The Right to Rectification: You may request that we correct any information you believe is inaccurate or complete information you believe is incomplete.
  • The Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten"): You may request that we erase your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • The Right to Restrict Processing: You have the right to request that we restrict the processing of your personal data.
  • The Right to Opt-Out of Sale: We do not and will not sell your personal data to third parties.

To exercise any of these rights, please submit a formal request to our privacy team using the contact information provided below. We will respond to all verifiable requests within 30 days.

6. Data Retention & Security Measures

We retain your email address and purchase history only for as long as it is necessary to provide you with ongoing access to your digital purchases (e.g., sending you a replacement download link) and to comply with our legal and tax obligations.

We implement robust, industry-standard technical and organizational security measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, loss, destruction, or alteration. While no internet transmission is 100% secure, we mandate strict security protocols, including HTTPS encryption across our site and reliance on highly vetted third-party vendors (like Lemon Squeezy) for sensitive processing.
